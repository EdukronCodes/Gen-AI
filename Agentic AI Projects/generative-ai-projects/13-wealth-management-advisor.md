# AI-Powered Wealth Management Advisor

## Introduction

The AI-Powered Wealth Management Advisor is a production-grade Generative AI system for retail banking that provides comprehensive wealth management services to high-net-worth clients. The system analyzes client portfolios, financial goals, risk tolerance, and market conditions to generate personalized investment strategies, tax optimization plans, and estate planning recommendations.

## Objective

- Provide personalized wealth management advice to high-net-worth clients
- Generate comprehensive investment strategies and portfolio recommendations
- Optimize tax strategies and estate planning
- Improve client engagement and satisfaction
- Support relationship managers with AI-powered insights
- Ensure compliance with SEC and FINRA regulations
- Scale wealth management services efficiently

## Technology Used

- **LLM Framework**: GPT-4 Turbo, Claude 3 Opus for financial reasoning
- **Portfolio Analytics**: QuantLib, Zipline for portfolio analysis
- **Market Data**: Bloomberg API, Refinitiv, Alpha Vantage
- **Tax Optimization**: Tax calculation engines, tax law databases
- **Risk Models**: Monte Carlo simulations, Modern Portfolio Theory
- **NLP**: spaCy, Transformers for document analysis
- **Vector Database**: Pinecone for financial knowledge base
- **ML Framework**: LangChain for orchestration
- **Backend**: Python 3.11+, FastAPI, Celery
- **Database**: PostgreSQL, TimescaleDB for time-series data
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS (EC2, RDS, S3)
- **Security**: Bank-level encryption, MFA, audit logging
- **Integration**: Custodial platforms, brokerage systems, tax software
- **Monitoring**: Datadog, CloudWatch, performance analytics

## Project Flow End to End

- **Wealth Assessment**: Collect comprehensive wealth information
